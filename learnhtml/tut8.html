BASIC Level: 2 HTML tutorial:

Great job getting this far! Ready for images? Images are used in websites to make them look better, make diagrams and a lot more! Follow these steps to make your image!

1. Download An Image.

2. Upload To Google Drive.

3. Open image in drive.google.com.

4. Open image in google drive.

5. Right click image and click "Copy Image Address".

6. Replace "Your Image Address Here" With The Image Address.

You are good to go!
